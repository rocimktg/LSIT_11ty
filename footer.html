The Five Tenets of Taekwondo

예의

Courtesy

Ye-wee

Courtesy, or 예의 (ye-wee), reminds us to show respect in the dojang and beyond through sincere etiquette and kindness.

염치

Integrity

Yom-chee

Integrity, or 염치 (yom-chee), calls for honesty, strong morals, and the courage to do what is right even when it is difficult.

인내

Perseverance

Een-nay

Perseverance, or 인내 (een-nay), is the willingness to keep pushing toward every goal despite obstacles in training or life.

극기

Self Control

Gook-gee

Self control, or 극기 (gook-gee), keeps thoughts, emotions, and actions in balance so we can respond with a steady mind and body.

백절불굴

Indomitable Spirit

Baekjool Boolgool

Indomitable spirit, or 백절불굴 (baekjool boolgool), to be defeated a hundred times yet never bent — the heart of true strength.
